Career Search/description - Computer Engineering

Description:

A computer engineer designs and develops computer hardware and software systems. They are responsible for user needs, finding solutions, create and test prototypes.

Education Required:

A degree in computer engineering is required for entry-level positions. Some employers may prefer candidates with a master's degree, especially for advanced positions.

Future Outlook:

The outlook for computer engineering careers is promising, with strong demand expected due to the increasing reliance on technology across various industries. As technology continues to evolve, there will be opportunities for computer engineers.

Salary Range:

The salary range for computer engineers varies depending on factors such as experience, education, location, and industry. For example, the average salary for Computer Engineer is $77,421 per year in the Toronto, ON. The average additional cash compensation for a Computer Engineer in the Toronto, ON is $2,332, with a range from $1,528 - $3,557.
