Pathway/Requirements - Computer Engineering

Educational Institution:

University of Ottawa, Faculty of Engineering

Entrance Requirements:

The entrance requirements for the Computer Engineering program at the University of Ottawa typically include prerequisites in mathematics, physics, and chemistry. Also, students need to meet specific grade requirements in these subjects and a average as a whole. This number changes every year and is never constant.

Description of First-Year Program (Courses):

The first-year program for Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa includes courses in mathematics, physics, and introductory engineering concepts.

Other Things Necessary to Complete the Degree Requirements:

In addition to completing the required courses, students pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa are sugested to participate in co-op work terms or internships to gain experience in the field.

Length of Time to Complete the Degree:

The standard length of time to complete a Bachelor (undergrade) in Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa is typically four years. However, this duration may vary depending on factors such as co-op work terms and grades.
